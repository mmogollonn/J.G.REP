Stream 2 — Drive-by field intelligence
iPhone · Otter.ai · Zapier · HubSpot
Employee-owned iPhone · $0 hardware cost

Property managers currently spend 45–90 minutes per drive-by on manual admin — typing notes, writing emails, creating maintenance requests. Observations get forgotten before reaching the office, and sales opportunities hidden inside routine inspections are never surfaced. This stack turns the iPhone already in their pocket into a complete field intelligence system that feeds directly into HubSpot.

Step-by-step workflow
1
Manager opens Otter.ai on their iPhone and taps record before entering the property. No setup, no equipment — the phone they already carry does everything.
2
Walks and talks. Speaks observations aloud as they inspect — roof condition, landscaping, interior issues. Otter transcribes in real time. iPhone camera documents damage or issues that need visual evidence.
3
Recording ends. Otter saves the full transcript to the shared workspace automatically. Manager walks away — no typing, no emails, no notes written.
4
Zapier detects the new recording, pulls the transcript, and routes it to the correct HubSpot property record — matched automatically by recording title set to the property address.
5
AI classifies severity. Cosmetic → note logged only. Maintenance-required → ticket created, vendor notified with details. Urgent → emergency flag raised, owner alerted immediately.
6
Sales intelligence trigger. Good condition + long-tenure owner → upsell flag sent to sales rep with owner details and suggested talking points. Drive-by becomes a lead source.
7
Owner receives inspection summary automatically drafted and sent. Professional, timestamped, zero writing required from the manager.
